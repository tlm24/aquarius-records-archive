Beloved Customers and Friends:

First off, heard the news while writing this, what is there to say about what just happened in Paris, it's awful. Our thoughts are with the people we know over there and everyone affected by this horrible violence. Makes it a little hard to say our usual, "Happy Friday!" - but let's find some happiness in this sometimes tragic world. For us, happiness has a lot to do with music, so on we go with this week's list, which is an in-betweener.

Last weeks' list proper, #487, is still quite current here - http://www.aquariusrecords.org/cat/newest.html - check it out please if you haven't, as tonight's in-between 'list' isn't really a list (not the usual kind anyway), it's a reminder about our big 45th birthday bash this weekend, coming up on Sunday the 15th!! Hopefully everybody reading this knows all about it already, but we've got a few more details for you below. We've been busy getting ready, and are really looking forward to it.

As mentioned before, to celebrate 45 (maybe 46, who can count?) years of 'getting the music to the people', aQuarius is having a big party. And SALE (details on that in a sec). This Sunday, November 15th from 11am to 9pm or so, with food, drink, DJing, birthday cake, lots of free goodies, raffle prizes, an aQ zine, a limited edition compilation, new tote bags, BBQ, and lots more. Let's break it down...

SALE: It's not just a party, but also a big sale! 20% off almost everything in the store! That's new and used vinyl, new cds, tapes, books, box sets, etc. And used cds are going to be 50% off!! (The only things not on sale are our new tote bags, aQ buttons, gift certificates and of course any items you might already have on hold here.) That's IN the store (not mailorder), on Sunday only, 11am - 9pm.

ZINE: aQ's Irwin has compiled a special b'day zine with your 'aQ stories' in it. So cool. Thanks everybody who contributed (one of whom, won the $45 gift certificate - Jon Arnold of San Francisco. Congrats!). And special thanks to Maria Forde who did the cover art (also our poster for the party). Grab a free copy here on Sunday!

AQ NEW WAVE TOTE BAGS: We just got brand new aQ tote bags printed for the party, with our vintage flaming A guitar "New Wave Music Is Our Specialty" design, as seen previously on our t-shirts. Limited to 50 totes total, in two color schemes - white on black, and red on 'natural'. They'll be $12.98 each, available Sunday in the shop. And we're gonna stuff 'em all with FREE goodies! For one thing, the first 40 totes sold will come with a copy of the 'Obscure Giants Of Acoustic Guitar' trading card set from Tompkins Square! (That's an $18 value right there, actually.) Plus all will have cds and other swag provided by a bunch of great labels we love thrown in!!!

DISCOUNT COUPON: All purchases in the store of $45 or more (before tax) will get a coupon good for 10% off your NEXT purchase!!

PRIZE RAFFLE: Anybody who makes any purchase in the store on Sunday will be entered into a raffle to win a prize package that we're still in the process of assembling, but will include a TON of neat stuff. We'll post the prize details on Instagram and Facebook tomorrow so you can see what you might win!!

LIVE AT AQ COMPILATION CD-R: Our super awesome customer Chris Allick put together a special, limited edition compilation cd-r for our 45th anniversary, featuring edits from live recordings he made of instore performances at aQ by James Blackshaw, 3 Leafs, Jooklo Duo, Lavender, Phil Manley & Isaiah Mitchell, Preston Swirnoff, and Father Murphy! It's limited to just 50 numbered copies, they'll be available at the party on Sunday, when you'll be able to get a copy FREE with any purchase of $60 or more, while supplies last. Big thanks to Chris and all the artists on the comp!!

GIVEAWAYS: Throughout the party, there will be random giveaways and freebies with purchases! Labels who provided giveaways and prizes for the raffle include Sublime Frequencies, Tompkins Square, Relapse, Fire, Rocket, Merge, The Flenser, Editions Mego, Secretly Canadian, Abduction, Thrill Jockey, Bureau B, Immune, Sub Pop, Hydra Head, Time Released Sound, Luaka Bop, Slumberland, Light In The Attic, Guerssen, and more!!! (Thanks guys!)

OUR BELOVED MAILORDER CUSTOMERS: We don't want to leave you out. All mail orders placed after this list goes out, through 12 midnight Sunday, will be entered in a separate raffle to win a special prize package which we're still figuring out, but it'll be full of cool stuff from some of the labels mentioned above. And also, all mailorders placed on Sunday of $60 or more will get a free copy of the live aQ instores cd-r comp, while supplies last. And, we'll make the new aQ totes available for purchase via mailorder after the party - assuming we still have 'em left. If you're interested, just let us know, and we'll grab you one on Monday morning (yes, still stuffed with swag from the party). As for the birthday zine, we'll put 'em in all mailorders we ship out after the party, while supplies last...

Probably forgot something, but that's plenty, right? Should be a fun party, and hopefully a busy day for the shop - we're kinda thinking of it as like a special aQuarius-only mini-Record Store Day. It kind of feels like it already, looking at all the boxes and boxes of records, cds and other goodies that a bunch of our label friends sent us to do those raffles and giveaways with!

The Facebook event page for the party is here: https://www.facebook.com/events/1662039347387864/

Oh, and if this Sunday wasn't exciting enough, there's ANOTHER special event we have planned here for the next Sunday - sound artist Robin The Fog (aka Howlround) from the UK will be doing a special tape-loop performance here at 6pm on November 22nd!

All right, thanks for your attention to this, and oh yeah of course take a look at the 'In Stock, Not Yet Reviewed' section below to see some things that have just showed up, including a new lp from The Heads, the vinyl of Coil's new Backwards album and the all-acoustic album by former Record Of The Weekers King Gizzard And The Lizard Wizard that came out today.

Also, fyi, a ton of newly arrived USED lps just got put out in the racks!

Ok, all local folks reading this, hope to see you all on Sunday (if not before - we're open Saturday too of course)!!

Btw, to view this on our website please go to: http://www.aquariusrecords.org/cat/4875.html

And as always, thanks for reading the list, passing it on to all your friends who love weird music, shopping at our store, turning -us- on to all sort of great stuff, and helping us spread the word and get all this great music to the people who love it. YOU!! And as always, please realize that we work really hard on the list, so if you find out about stuff through us, please try to buy your records from us. That way we can keep on doing what we do, and we'll always be here with our ears to the ground, and with cds full of metalcore pitbulls, death metal parrots, gamelan playing elephants, recordings of glaciers cracking, ice melting, zamboni's, life support systems, drag races, audience applause, and of course self flagellating Norwegian dwarves, moaning telephone wires, recorded exorcisms, acapella straight edge metalcore, high school battles of the bands, movie theater organ music, Christian psychedelic folk, Bhangra Black Sabbath as well as all the metal, indie rock, electronica, punk rock, reggae, dub, sixties psych, krautrock, classic rock, country and anything else your heart may desire. So thanks. A bunch!

[1] We will contact you to verify your order and let you know when it will be shipped. Please note that occasionally it may take a day or two for us to reply. We are not a faceless bunch of computers replying to your order -- we are human beings!

[2] If we are out of some of your items and we think we will get them within the same week, we can wait to ship. Or... If it's going to be more than a few days to complete your order, we will ship what we have and then will contact you as the remainders arrive.

[ note ] Due to the everchanging nature of the independent record business, we are not responsible for listed price changes (due to supplier price changes) and often cannot update our site fast enough to reflect these changes, but we will always try to let you know of any differences.

INTERNATIONAL SHIPPING :

-------------------------------- For foreign customers we ship via US AIRMAIL ("First Class International"). Your price is based on the actual cost of shipping. You can check the US Postal Service international rate calculator: http://ircalc.usps.com/. (Use the "Package" category and see the price for "First Class International". 1-3 cds is usually 1 pound. One LP is generally between 1.25 and 1.5 pounds depending on the record)

We highly recommend insurance for your international package, but it is very expensive (See below)! You can check the US Postal Service international rate calculator: http://ircalc.usps.gov/. (In that case, use the "Package" category and see the price for "Priority Mail International". Again, 1-3 cds is usually 1 pound. 1 lp is usually between 1.25 and 1.5 pounds.)

INTERNATIONAL INSURANCE :

-------------------------------- You are hereby forewarned that Aquarius is not responsible if your international package gets lost in the mail. Insurance is your only recourse if your records never show up. We very rarely experience *confirmed* permanently lost mail, but insurance might provide some additional piece of mind. We strongly recommend it. But yes, it is very expensive. Even moreso recently with the recent hike in USPS international shipping rates, but still, it's your choice. Again: Aquarius is not responsible for lost mail, so if you aren't willing to take a (slight but real) risk, please buy the insurance.

International insurance is very expensive! In fact often the insurance costs more than the value of your package, in which case it obviously does not make sense to insure it. You can check the US Postal Service international rate calculator: http://ircalc.usps.gov/. (Use the "Package" category and see the price for "Priority Mail International", which is the way insured packages are sent. 1-3 cds is usually 1 pound. 1 lp is usually a between 1.25 and 1.5 pounds.)

For example: for a one-pound package worth $25 going to England, shipping without insurance is about $16.75. But with insurance, the shipping / insurance total is over $42! Go up to two pounds and uninsured first class is about $24, insured priority over $46. But bigger packages weighing over four pounds automatically get bumped up to the priority rate, in which case insurance is generally only a few dollars more and in that case definitely worth it.

It is your responsibility to check the international rate calculator in order to determine whether or not you want international insurance. If you tell us you want international insurance, we will add it to your order no matter how much it costs!

PAYMENT :

-------------------------------- We accept the following credit cards: Visa, MC, Discover, and Amex. We will not charge your credit card until your order is ready to ship.

Money orders are accepted, but only in $USD. International customers please note that they must be international postal money orders purchased at a post office. Additional processing fees may apply.

If you wish to pay by money order, you must confirm the order with us through email or phone BEFORE you send any payment. It's best to just use the secure order form on the website, and when checking out mark money order as your payment choice. We will then process your order and respond with all the crucial payment info.

We also accept payment by Paypal. If you opt for this payment method, we will send you a paypal total and payment instructions as soon as we've confirmed your order with you. Please do not send payment until you have received human contact from our mailorder department.

Unfortunately, we cannot take personal checks for mailorder, sorry!
