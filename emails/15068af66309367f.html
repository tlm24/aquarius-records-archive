Announcement: aQuarius recOrds 45th b'day PARTY & SALE & ZINE coming

up!!! ******

Hey folks! As we mentioned on the list last week, we're planning a big

party at the store next month to celebrate our 45th anniversary! Can

you believe it?

Well, actually, it might be our 46th, our current motto, after all, is

"the store that's old enough not to remember exactly how old it is"...

We've been around since either 1970, or maybe 1969, some folks

recently told us. The elders here at the store were all juset born

sometime around then, so can't rightly recall. Anyway, we celebrated

our 40th five years ago, and had our 30th party fifteen years ago so

figured we should celebrate our 45th before this year is out.

So, please come join us here at aQuarius on Sunday November the 15th,

for a party that will last all day (and into the night), from 11am to

9pm. There will be a store-wide sale, DJs, a special aQ anniversary

zine, new limited edition aQ totes for sale, food and drink, good

times in other words! We're looking at it like a mini aQuarius-only

Record Store Day, if you will.

The storewide sale will feature some steep discounts on all vinyl and

cds in the store - and also, everyone who makes $45 purchase or more

will get a coupon for another 10% off their next purchase at the shop.

The DJs spinning music will include not only a bunch of your friendly

aQ staffers of course, but also some awesome pals of ours, among 'em

WOBBLY, JOSH CHEON (Dark Entries/Honey Soundsystem), CHASMS, CHRIS

MCVICKER (Swiftumz), DJ MATOKIE (KALX), DJ PRAJMETAL (She Likes It

Heavy), JOSH POLLOCK (DJ Female Convict Scorpion/3 Leafs), and RYAN

POULSEN (Booty Bassment)! We'll post the set schedule of who's on

when, when we have it all sorted out.

VOODOO VAN will be parked outside part of the day if you're in the

mood for their delicious sandos, tacos, and fried food.

And later on Andee will be running a grill outside too, so BYOBBQ!

So, please mark your calendars, and tell your friends!! The more

people who show up the more fun it will be. The Facebook event page is

here: https://www.facebook.com/events/1662039347387864/

Below, you'll find the press release (share with anyone you think

should know about it!) and also a call for entries for the zine we're

doing - again, please share that with any of your aQ loving'

friends... It's also a way for all of your far-flung mail order

customers, who can't make it to the party, to participate, we'd very

much appreciate your written contributions to the zine if you feel so

inclined! Thanks!!!!

Oh, and while we have your attention don't forget the two events

happening at aQ much sooner, THIS coming weekend. There's our Litcrawl

presentation on Saturday October 17th from 8:30 to 9:30 featuring JOSH

ROSENTHAL, author of The Record Store Of The Mind (and boss of the

Tompkins Square record label) with special guest POWELL ST. JOHN.

Powell played with The 13th Floor Elevators and Janis Joplin back in

the day, and will be sharing stories with Josh - and maybe playing a

song or two. Facebook: https://www.facebook.com/events/1707957086092602/

And then on Sunday the 18th, which is also a "Sunday Streets" on

Valencia, the wonderful DOUG HREAM BLUNT, responsible for former

aQuarius Record Of The Week release "Gentle Persuasion", will be here

from 4-5pm doing a record signing of Luaka Bop's new collection of his

work, "My Name Is Doug Hream Blunt", out this Friday. Please come down

and meet Doug! FB: https://www.facebook.com/events/545056598974703/

Ok, that's it for now. Look out for our regularly scheduled "in-

between list" on Friday. Thanks!

-Andee, Allan, Irwin, Scott, Jim, Kirk, Nick, Harry, and Sydney

FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE: aQuarius recOrds to celebrate their 45th

anniversary with an all day birthday bash at the shop, one of the

nation's oldest and most iconic record stores!

Sunday, November 15, 2015 (San Francisco, CA) - Save the date for the

45th anniversary party of one of the oldest, most eclectic and well

respected record stores in the country, aQuarius recOrds. The party

will be all day, at the store, from 11am-7pm, with live DJ sets, a

storewide sale, snacks, drinks and special limited edition items for

sale.

Since its beginnings in 1970, aQuarius has always been a vibrant and

supportive hub for the Bay Area music community, champions of

underground sounds and counter-culture music. Its original location,

located next door to Harvey Milk's camera shop in the Castro, was one

of the first stores in the country to sell punk rock records, and was

an important meeting place for the early San Francisco punk scene.

Through its move to Noe Valley and finally to its current location in

the Mission on Valencia street in the mid '90s, aQuarius has served as

a sanctuary for music lovers not only in San Francisco but across the

globe, thanks to the store's famous, biweekly "New Arrivals List" that

goes out to thousands of subscribers worldwide (archived on their

website at www.aquariusrecords.org). With a dedicated and passionate

staff that writes extensive, opinionated reviews of all the albums it

carries, aQuarius has become the go-to spot for weird, unique, obscure

and esoteric music that often can't be found anywhere else.

Over the years aQuarius has also hosted numerous instore performances

by the likes of Yo La Tengo, Neutral Milk Hotel, Cluster, Elliott

Smith, Circle, Death Cab For Cutie, Jonathan Richman, Superchunk,

Matmos,The Mountain Goats, Nada Surf, The Olivia Tremor Control, Sir

Richard Bishop, James Blackshaw, Josef Van Wissem, Grouper, Juliana

Barwick, Kid Koala, Wavves, Tetuzi Akiyama, Iron & Wine, The Posies,

and tons more!

"aQuarius to us has always felt to us, sort of like a massive,

constantly expanding mixtape, and every New Arrivals list is just more

crazy music we get to add to it, and then we get to share with all our

friends and customers. aQuarius strives to not just be a local record

store for people in the neighborhood, but also a local record store

for people in places without their own local record store.

We feel so lucky that we've been able to share so much amazing music,

and to have connected with so many fantastic friends, customers and

artists all over the world. Thanks for helping us keep aQ alive and

kicking!"

-Andee Connors, co-owner

With all the rapid changes happening in our city, and especially here

in the ever evolving Mission district, the fact that aQuarius is still

standing is a victory unto itself, and it remains a special place for

all the music nerds, record obsessed weirdos and music lovers still

holding on in San Francisco, as well as all our faithful customers,

all over the world.

CALL FOR ZINE SUBMISSIONS: A special zine to commemorate the 45th

b'day of aQuarius records!

aQ is turning 45!

For so many of us aQ has been a place where we have discovered new

music, seen amazing performances, made new friends, argued over

records, shared our passion, bought a record that changed us in some

way, read a review that excited or repulsed us, tripped out over an

interaction we witnessed, had a conversation that has stayed with us,

had a missed connection, found the one we love, etc....write about it!

or use aQ as your location for a short piece of fiction/creative non-

fiction

we're collecting your aQ stories for a zine to be released on our 45th

b'day party on November 15th.

please keep your stories/memories/reflections under 500 words.

all submissions will be entered into a raffle to win a $45 gift

certificate to use at aQ!

email submission to: aq45@aquariusrecords.org

and SAVE THE DATE to celebrate with us in the store on Sunday,
