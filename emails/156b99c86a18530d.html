Goodbyes & What's Next after aQ: Book, Blog, Party...!

August 23, 2016 — New Arrivals #None
← Back to Archive

Beloved Customers & Friends:

Hey everybody! Long time, no email... well, of course that's because aQuarius is not aQuarius any more, it's now Stranded. Our handpicked successors took over and opened up at our old location last month, after doing some major remodeling and adding a ton of vinyl to the inventory. The transition was an amazingly rapid one, lots to do and not a lot of time to reflect. But now that Stranded is up and running - their new website, https://www.strandedrecords.com , just went live, please check it out - we wanted to send out one last farewell email to say 'bye'!

As we said before, this decision to stop doing aQuarius was a necessary one (financially) but NOT an easy one. We miss it - and all of you - very much already. But things had to change, and so they did, hopefully for the best. Several of the aQ staff can still be found behind the counter at Stranded, though, and former aQ-overlords Allan & Andee are now able to pursue other projects - which include doing (at long, long last!) a big book of selected aQ reviews from our five hundred and two aQ New Arrivals lists, 1995-2016! Hopefully you're all as excited about that as we are, we're imagining a hefty tome as a fitting memorial to the shop, something to treasure for all of you who've told us your Saturday mornings are just not the same anymore without the aQ-list to peruse.

We've set up a new website, called "Why...Do You Like It?" at http://www.whydoyoulikeit.com (named after a common catchphrase we had here at aQ, a more in-depth explanation can be found here: http://www.whydoyoulikeit.com/p/about.html ), where we'll be blogging to keep folks updated on the progress of this print project, as well as other after-aQ related stuff. If you go there you can hear about what's going on with the aQ-reviews book as we figure it out (there may be a crowdfunding campaign coming up) - and also we'll be posting NEW reviews and other things about stuff that we've been digging.

The hope is that our blog will, eventually, kind of be a continuation of what we loved doing at aQuarius, with the gushingly lengthy reviews and way-more-than-ten top ten lists, and all that good stuff. Just sans actual store. Treating it as more of a hobby, for now. It's sort of a 'beta' site at the moment so lots will change but we've already posted a couple reviews, with more to come. Let's see what develops... And we're going to have an occasional email newsletter for the book & blog too, you can sign up here: http://tinyletter.com/whydoyoulikeit

Also, local folks, we still intend to have a big aQ farewell party too - we had to push it back a bit 'cause of how much work the aQ-to-Stranded transition has turned out to be, but it'll happen and you're invited - we'll ask the Stranded folks to let you know about it on their email list when planning is completed. Also, if you follow us via the "Why...Do You Like It?" website and/or email list we'll keep you informed about it. We think we've got Tony Molina committed to doing an all-Ovens set at the party, btw!

Oh, and also in nostalgia-for-aQ news, our filmmaker pal Kenneth, who most recently made the Old Man Gloom documentary Here Is A Gift For You, is finishing up his documentary all about aQuarius!! Should be released by the end of next year. Can't wait to see it, though we're sure to be a little bit embarrassed. More info: http://www.thescourge.com/blog/2016/8/15/it-came-from-aquarius-records

So we hope you'll all join us at our new blog, fans of aQ will definitely feel right at home, and we really do wanna keep in touch with everybody, customers, labels, bands, friends and strangers.

All right, better wrap it up, we hate long, sad goodbyes... we love you all and hope to still be part of your music-obsessed lives in some form in the future. Super massive heartfelt thanks to all old aQ customers and all our former aQ co-workers. And best of luck to our friends at Stranded!

xo,

Andee & Allan
