Hey folks,

Just a reminder that, tomorrow, we'll be hosting a very special art

opening / reception / party for our friends Secret Serpents, who

besides making amazing posters, also run the equally awesome Valley

King label, who've released records by Carlton Melton, Spiders, Golden

Void, Pins Of Light, Anywhere, Sweet Apple and more! We'll have just

some of Secret Serpents past and present tour poster series on display

and for sale (including High On Fire, Mudhoney, Jesus Lizard, Unsane,

Dinosaur Jr, Pavement, Faith No More, the Melvins, Swans, Graveyard

and loads more)! And this will be an ongoing, changing, show, with

different posters up all the time. All poster purchases on the opening

night will come with free silkscreened handbills, art prints,

stickers, or anything else we can dig up! Plus, Alan Forbes, Junko

Mizuno, and John Howard will be on hand to hang out, shoot the shit,

and to sign your posters!

In addition to all that, there'll also be a live in-store set by

ISAIAH MITCHELL (Earthless / Golden Void / Black Elk Medicine Band),

not to mention complimentary beer from 101 North Brewing Company!!

Here are the crucial details:
